About the Event

The Christian Tech Conference is a unique event that merges faith with technological innovation. It aims to gather Christian young adults, professionals, and tech enthusiasts to explore how technology can be used to glorify God and enhance communities, while also fostering ethical innovation.

The event will feature keynote speakers, panel discussions, tech workshops, and Christian-themed hackathons. Workshops will cover practical topics such as app development for local churches and discussions on AI ethics from a biblical viewpoint. The conference seeks to motivate Christians to confidently engage with modern and rapidly evolving technology without compromising their faith.

What to Expect

Integrating Faith and Technology
